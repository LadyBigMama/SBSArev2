Giving Back to Our Mountains

It’s easy to take for granted the incredible places we get to fly from, so the Santa Barbara Soaring Association recently stepped up and committed time and resources to cleaning up our local launch sites in the Santa Barbara mountains. We are dedicated to preserving these breathtaking locations for not only our pilots, but for everyone who enjoys the mountains. A big thank you to everyone who helped out, your efforts are what made this possible!
