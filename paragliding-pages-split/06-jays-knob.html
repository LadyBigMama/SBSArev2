Fly at your own risk


Thermal Flying with some ridge soaring

Rating: P4

Appropriate winds: SE

Radio Frequency: 144.310

Used occasionally as an alternative launch by experienced pilots in strong South East.

If you're going XC, you should install our DNL file. It is sensitive and only shared with visitors upon request. Hop on Telegram, introduce yourself, and someone will gladly share it with you.

Launch

Launching Jay’s Knob is committing. There is no good landing, strong wind technic and strong thermal technic are needed. In strong SE, one can potentially ridge soar the site to get high and transition to the Thermal Factory trigger.
